# English Check — Ernest

**Time:** about 30 minutes · **On your own** — no dictionary, no help.

**How it works:** one answer per question, don't go back and change earlier ones. For the Russian sentences, write the most *natural* English — not word-by-word. If you really don't know, write **PASS** and move on (that's fine — it just tells us what to practise). The last task is a short piece of writing.

*(No marking as you go — we'll go through it together afterwards.)*

*Note: correct answers are spread across a/b/c — there is no "always pick a" pattern.*
