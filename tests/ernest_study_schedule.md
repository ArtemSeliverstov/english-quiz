# Ernest — English Plan Before the Entrance Test 📘

Hey Ernest — good news first: on the practice test you scored **82%**, and your prepositions, word order, plurals and basic tenses were **all 100%**. You don't need to study those. 💪

This plan just tightens **three things** that slipped. About **30 minutes a day**. That's it.

Everything you need is in the **prep sheet** (`ernest_entrance_prep_deep_dives.md`) — this schedule just tells you what to do each day.

---

## 🔑 Two rules that matter most

1. **Write your answers — don't just pick a/b/c.** You're already good at spotting the right answer. The test wants you to *produce* it, so practise by writing full sentences.
2. **On the real exam, check your work twice at the end:** one read looking *only* at **a / the**, one read looking *only* at **verb tenses**. This alone catches most mistakes.

---

## 📅 The plan

*(If your test is sooner than 4 days, just do Day 1 and Day 2 — they matter most.)*

### Day 1 — Conditionals (the "would have" fix)
- [ ] Read **Deep Dive 1** in the prep sheet
- [ ] Do the 6 practice sentences (write them out), then check answers
- [ ] Write 6–8 sentences: *"If you could change one thing about your new school, what would it be?"*
- [ ] Reread your writing — did you use **would + verb** for now, and keep **will/would out of the "if" part**?

### Day 2 — Articles (when to use *a*, *the*, or nothing)
- [ ] Read **Deep Dive 2**
- [ ] Do the 8 practice sentences, then check answers
- [ ] Write 6–8 sentences: *describe your room* (this makes you use lots of *a* and *the*)
- [ ] Reread — no *"a"* in front of things like *advice / information / homework*, and *"the"* for specific things

### Day 3 — Present perfect (*for* / *since* / *have you ever*)
- [ ] Read **Deep Dive 3**
- [ ] Do the 6 practice sentences, then check answers
- [ ] Write 6–8 sentences: *"Three things you have done this year, and how long you have had your favourite thing."*
- [ ] Reread — did you use **have / has + the third verb form** with *for* and *since*?

### Day 4 — Test eve (light day)
- [ ] **Redo the first practice test** (all 22 questions) and see if you beat 82%
- [ ] Skim the three Deep Dive summaries one more time
- [ ] **No new studying tonight.** Get a good sleep. 😴

---

## ✅ On test day
- Read each question carefully — don't rush.
- Answer everything (a guess beats a blank).
- Save the **last 3–4 minutes** for your two checks: **a/the**, then **verb tenses**.

You've got a solid base already. This is just polishing. You've got this. 🚀
